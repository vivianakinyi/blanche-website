About Bluche Hospitality

With headquarters based in Nairobi, Kenya, Bluche Hospitality Consultants and Supplies Services provide superior consulting services in Hospitality Management serving both the domestic and international markets. Bluche Hospitality Consultants and Supplies Services bring a fresh and innovative approach to consulting services, taking the service industry a notch higher.

Our goal is to exceed the expectations of every client by offering outstanding customer service, increased flexibility, and greater value thus improving operation efficiency. Our consultants are distinguished by their functional and technical expertise combined with their hands-on experience in the hospitality industry coupled by extraordinary attention to detail, team work and service thereby ensuring that our clients receive the most effective and professional service.,

Our Mission

As experts in the hospitality industry, Bluche Hospitality Consultants and Supplies Services is involved in every stage of the hospitality training process from planning through to execution and follow up in hospitality training and human resource recruitment thus offering continual functional and technical support. Our extensive skills encompass all aspects of implementation and operation, including hospitality requirements, development of functional specifications for client approval and overseeing development teams to meet the client needs.

Unlike other consulting firms, at Bluche Hospitality Consultants and Supplies Services, we posses hands on operations experience where our consultants are well versed in all aspects of the hospitality industry from training, budgeting , productivity to establishing and maintaining service standards. As a young company we pride ourselves on our proven track record for effectively consulting in the industry for upcoming and existing hotels and restaurants. This is due to effectively directing our consultants in the development of training material to ensure that all business requirements are met within budget restraints and time schedules.
